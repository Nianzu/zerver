Hoverpen

Inspired by the Novium line of hoverpens, this DIY hoverpen is a conversation starter! Using commonly available magnets and the beloved Pilot G2 pen core, this design won't break the bank or your wrist. The design uses 1 (one) 3mm x 2mm magnet, and 38 (thirty eight) 8mm x 2mm magnets. The magnets don't have to be incredibly strong, I purchased a cheap magnet kit off amazon, and have many magnets left over. Assembly is easy, but may require light taps with a hammer to set the magnets correctly. All the magnets are oriented the same way!

Humming Bird Feeder Balcony Hangar

Hang your humming bird feeder or any other balcony decoration with this stylish hanger! Made from Laser cut galvanized steel, this is sure to weather any storm.

Sprite Extruder Cover

Protect your new Sprite Extruder from dust with this NASA inspired dust cover! Attaches with 2 magnets at the top to allow easy access. Vents allow for normal airflow.
